Anthony Ryabchikov

Studio Artist BFA · Business & Liberal Arts Minor · Japanese Minor — Fall 2026    gamboiuwu  /  ギャム

New York–based freelance character artist, event director, and creative ecosystem builder. Ten-plus years in studio art with a specialization in kemono and anthro character design, digital illustration, Telegram sticker packs, and 2D animation. Work is technically precise and emotionally resonant — built in Clip Studio Paint, Adobe Creative Cloud, Blender, and After Effects, rooted in traditional drawing and color theory.

Founder of New York Furs — an independent not-for-profit with 160+ events including Phoenix Bark (NYC's largest furry bar event, 100+ monthly) and Fuwa Furry Fest — and CEO of Yuka Designs, a futuristic eco-friendly techwear label. Award-winning game developer (Resonance Awards 2021: Most Innovative Project & Best Sound Design). Drawing instructor at Anthro Art Academy. Music composer as letfexraut.

02 // STICKERS
Telegram Sticker Packs

Expressive sticker packs built for Telegram — simple chibi flats and complex rendered sets. Packs of 1, 3, 5, or 10. No NSFW upcharge. Fast turnaround.
