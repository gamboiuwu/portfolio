About

Anthony Ryabchikov

Studio Artist BFA · Business & Liberal Arts Minor · Japanese Minor — Fall 2026    gamboiuwu

New York–based freelance artist, designer, and event director specializing in character design, digital illustration, animation, and videography. With over ten years in studio art and a background spanning Adobe Creative Cloud, 2D animation, and Blender, Anthony brings a technically rigorous and deeply personal voice to every commission.

Founder of New York Furs — an independent not-for-profit running monthly community events including Phoenix Bark and Fuwa Furry Fest — and CEO of Yuka Designs, an eco-friendly techwear label built on sustainable materials and in-house artistry. Award-winning game developer (Resonance Awards 2021).
